Installation

Solar Derby is an exciting competition where student teams design, build, and race their own solar-powered remote control cars. With speeds of up to 30 miles per hour, the cars are put to the test in a 1-hour-long derby race and a high-speed drag race. The students are responsible for every aspect of the car's creation, including design, construction, testing, and tuning. This hands-on experience not only promotes teamwork and problem-solving skills but also provides a practical understanding of engineering, physics, electronics, and electrical engineering. By building cars that must be both lightweight and durable, students develop critical thinking and practical skills that will serve them well in their future careers. Participating in Solar Derby is an excellent way for students to gain experience and knowledge in a fun and engaging way. It encourages creativity, innovation, and a passion for learning that will stay with them for a lifetime.
