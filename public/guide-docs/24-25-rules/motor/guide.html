With motors for your solar roller there are 2 main types of motors, brushed and brushless. A brushless motor uses timed magnets to spin, and a brushed motor uses currents forced through brushes the spin. Brushless motors have an advantage over brushed motors in terms of heat generation running cooler, but brushed motors have a starting advantage with more torque at zero RPM.


RPM

"RPM" stands for revolutions per minute and is how fast something is spinning.
